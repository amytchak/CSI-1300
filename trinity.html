Trinity University

Trinity University is located in San Antionio, Texas.

I have enjoyed my time at Trinity so far as a freshman. I love the small and friendly community. The class size is perfect for me and I love San Antonio. San Antonio is close enough to my home town, Houston, that I can drive home in 3 hours if I need. Yet I am far enough to be independant and do the things I want to.
